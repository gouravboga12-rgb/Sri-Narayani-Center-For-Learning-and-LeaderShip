About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the driving force behind the Sri Narayani Center for Learning and Leadership.

Our Founder

Mr. Ravikanth Perukulambil

With a profound commitment to education and leadership, Mr. Ravikanth Perukulambil has dedicated his career to transforming learning environments. His experience in leadership roles has shaped a unique curriculum that not only imparts knowledge but builds character.

He believes that absolute dedication, combined with modern pedagogical strategies, can unlock the immense potential within every educator and professional.

"Education is not merely about transmitting facts; it's about igniting curiosity and empowering individuals to become leaders in their own right."

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence that revolutionizes education and shapes visionary leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To nurture excellence in both education and the corporate world by shaping inspiring educators and empowering leaders with the tools to implement change effectively.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Excellence. We believe in nurturing talent and pushing boundaries.
